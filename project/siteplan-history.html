History

The Gravity Jet Suit was invented by British entrepreneur and former Royal Marine Reservist Richard Browning, who founded Gravity Industries in March 2017 to commercialize the technology. Development began in early 2016, with the first successful test flight occurring in November 2016. The suit uses five micro gas turbines to achieve vertical take-off and landing (VTOL) and is controlled by the pilot's body movements.
