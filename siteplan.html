Site Plan - Kevin Noorda

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase my skills and projects as I learn dynamic web development in the WDD 131 course.

Audience

The primary audience for this website includes potential employers, classmates, and instructors interested in my progress and projects.

Content

  • Home Page: Introduction and overview of the site.
  • Projects: A portfolio of completed assignments and projects.
  • Specifications: Articles and reflections on what I've learned.

Structure

The website will have a simple navigation menu at the top, allowing users to easily access different sections. Each section will be clearly defined with headings and subheadings.

Site Name

Kevin Noorda Portfolio — This name clearly identifies the site as my personal portfolio and reflects its purpose of showcasing my web development projects and skills.

Scenarios

  • What is the Gravity suit used for?
  • Why is this interesting?

Color Scheme

  • Green (#1f7a1f): Used for the header and footer to give the site a professional, grounded feel.
  • Teal Accent (#2dd4bf): Used for hover effects and section accents to add visual interest.

Typography

Arial, sans-serif is used for all body text and headings to ensure readability and a clean, modern appearance.
